Tribute to System of a Down (SOAD)

My favorite band

Here is a timeline of the band

  • 1992 - They meet and form a band named Soil.
  • 1994 - Soil split and they form a new band "System of a Down".
  • 1998 - They release their first self titled album.
  • 2001 - They release "Toxicity" and "Steal this album!".
  • 2004 - Band hiatus
  • 2010 - Reounion and touring
  • 2020 - Posible new album!
